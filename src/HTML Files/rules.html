Game Rules

How to Play

  • Player 1 uses the X symbol, and Player 2 or AI uses the O symbol.
  • Click on an empty box to make your move.
  • The first player to align three symbols in a row, column, or diagonal wins the game.
  • If all boxes are filled and no one wins, the game ends in a draw.

Important Notes

  • You cannot select a box that has already been marked.
  • Take your time to strategize your moves!
  • Have fun and aim for victory!

Good Luck! Let the best player win!
